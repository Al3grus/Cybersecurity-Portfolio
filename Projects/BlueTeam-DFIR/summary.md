## Task Details:

### 1. Initial Assessment:
	- Identify any unusual or unauthorized activities;
	- Establish a timeline of events.

### 2. Analysis:
	- Determine the methods used by the attacker to gain access;
	- Which files were accessed or extracted?

### 3. Reporting:
	- Summarize findings:
		▪ How the attacker gained access.
		▪ The timeline of the attacker's activities.
		▪ Potential vulnerabilities exploited during the attack.
		▪ Recommendations for preventing similar incidents in the future.
